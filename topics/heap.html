Heap

  • A nearly complete binary tree
  • Min heap: parent <= child
  • Max heap: parent >= child
Min heap:
     5
   /   \
  8      9
 / \    /
10  12 15

Represented as an array:
[5,8,9,10,12,15]

Max heap:
     20
   /   \
  16    17
 / \    /
7   5  2
Represented as an array:
[20,16,17,7,5,2]

Find parent index of i:

floor((i - 1) / 2)

Find left child index of i:

i * 2 + 1

Find right child index of i:

i * 2 + 2
